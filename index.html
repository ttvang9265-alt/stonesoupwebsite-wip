About Stone Soup Fresno

Stone Soup Fresno is a community benefit organization located in Fresno, CA. We are dedicated to providing resources, education, and support to families in our community.

Through our preschool, playgroup, and cultural programs, we help children and families realize their full potential. Our programs are designed to nurture growth, celebrate diversity, and build strong community bonds.

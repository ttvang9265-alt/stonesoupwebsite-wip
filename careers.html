Work With Us

Stone Soup Fresno is always interested in connecting with passionate individuals who want to make a difference in our community. We are a community benefit organization dedicated to inspiring children and nurturing families.

We believe in creating a supportive, inclusive workplace where team members can grow professionally while contributing to our mission. If you are interested in early childhood education, community support, or nonprofit work, we want to hear from you.

Why Stone Soup?

  • Be part of a mission-driven organization
  • Support children and families in the Fresno community
  • Work in a collaborative, team-oriented environment
  • Professional development and growth opportunities
  • Make a meaningful impact every day

Preschool Teacher

Full-Time
📍 Fresno, CA
💼 Education
📅 Immediate Opening

We are seeking a creative, nurturing, and dedicated Preschool Teacher to join our team. The ideal candidate will implement developmentally appropriate curriculum, create a safe and engaging learning environment, and partner with families to support each child's growth.

Responsibilities

  • Plan and implement age-appropriate curriculum aligned with California Early Learning Standards
  • Create a warm, inclusive classroom environment
  • Assess and document children's developmental progress
  • Communicate regularly with families about their child's progress
  • Supervise classroom assistants and volunteers
  • Maintain a safe, clean, and organized classroom

Qualifications

  • Associate's or Bachelor's degree in Early Childhood Education (ECE) or related field
  • 12+ ECE units required; 24+ units preferred
  • Minimum 1 year experience working with preschool-aged children
  • Current CPR/First Aid certification (or willingness to obtain)
  • Clear background check and TB test
  • Bilingual (English/Hmong) preferred but not required
$18 - $24/hour (based on experience & education) Apply Now

Teacher Assistant / Aide

Part-Time
📍 Fresno, CA
💼 Education
📅 Immediate Opening

We're looking for enthusiastic Teacher Assistants to support our lead teachers in providing high-quality early education. This is a great entry-level opportunity for those interested in pursuing a career in early childhood education.

Responsibilities

  • Assist lead teacher with daily activities and classroom management
  • Supervise children during play, meals, and transitions
  • Prepare materials and set up learning centers
  • Help maintain a clean and safe classroom environment
  • Support children with personal care needs as required

Qualifications

  • High school diploma or equivalent required
  • 6+ ECE units preferred
  • Previous experience working with young children preferred
  • Current CPR/First Aid certification (or willingness to obtain)
  • Clear background check and TB test
$15 - $18/hour Apply Now

IT Support Technician

Full-Time
📍 Fresno, CA
💼 Technology
📅 Open Until Filled

Stone Soup Fresno is seeking an IT Support Technician to maintain and support our technology infrastructure. The ideal candidate will have experience with Windows systems, network administration, and security systems.

Responsibilities

  • Administer Genetec Security Center video surveillance and access control systems
  • Manage Active Directory, group policies, and Office 365 accounts
  • Troubleshoot hardware, software, and network issues
  • Install and configure security cameras, cabling, and network infrastructure
  • Deploy endpoint protection and anti-malware solutions
  • Provide technical support to staff and volunteers

Qualifications

  • Associate's degree in Information Technology, Computer Science, or related field
  • 1+ years experience in IT support or system administration
  • Experience with Windows Server, Active Directory, and Office 365
  • Knowledge of security systems (Genetec preferred) and IP cameras
  • Experience with CAT6/CAT7 cabling and network infrastructure
  • Strong problem-solving and communication skills
$20 - $28/hour (based on experience) Apply Now

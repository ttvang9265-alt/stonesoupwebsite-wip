Eligibility & Fees

Learn about enrollment requirements and financial assistance options.

🎯 Eligibility Information

Stone Soup Fresno serves families in the Fresno community. To learn about specific eligibility requirements for each program, please contact us directly.

Programs Available

  • Preschool: Early education program for young children
  • Playgroup: Play-based learning experiences
  • Learn Hmong: Cultural and language programs

Required Information

When you contact us about enrollment, we will provide you with a complete list of required documentation. Generally, you will need to provide:

  1. Completed enrollment application form
  2. Proof of child's age (birth certificate or passport)
  3. Up-to-date immunization records
  4. Proof of residency
  5. Any additional documents as requested

💰 Tuition & Financial Assistance

We believe quality early education should be accessible to all families. We offer sliding scale tuition and financial assistance based on family income and size.

Financial Assistance Available

Stone Soup Fresno offers scholarships and financial assistance to qualifying families. We accept various subsidy programs and work with families to ensure our programs are accessible.

Payment Options

  • Sliding scale tuition based on income
  • Scholarships for qualifying families
  • Flexible payment plans
  • Acceptance of childcare subsidies

📝 How to Apply

To begin the enrollment process, please follow these steps:

1

Contact Us

Reach out by phone or email to express interest and request enrollment information.

2

Schedule a Tour

Visit our facility to meet our team and see our programs in action.

3

Complete Application

Fill out the provided application form and gather required documents.

4

Submit Documents

Return your completed application and documents as instructed by our staff.

⚠️ Important Notice

We do not accept enrollment applications through this website.

To request an application or for more information about our programs, please:

  • Call us during business hours
  • Email us
  • Visit our facility in person
  • Message us through Facebook

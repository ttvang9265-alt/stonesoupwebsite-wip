Eligibility & Fees

Learn about our enrollment requirements, tuition fees, and financial assistance options.

🎯 Eligibility Requirements

Stone Soup Fresno serves families in the Fresno community. To ensure we can provide the best care and education for your child, please review the following eligibility requirements:

Age Requirements

  • Preschool: Children ages 3-5 years old
  • Playgroup: Children ages 2-5 years old
  • Learn Hmong: Open to all ages, families welcome

Required Documentation

The following documents are required to complete your child's enrollment:

  1. Completed enrollment application form
  2. Child's birth certificate or passport
  3. Up-to-date immunization records
  4. Proof of residency (utility bill, lease agreement)
  5. Income verification (for sliding scale tuition)
  6. Emergency contact information
  7. Medical/dental examination forms
  8. Signed parent handbook acknowledgment

Health Requirements

  • All children must be up-to-date on required immunizations
  • Annual physical examination required
  • Children must be potty-trained for preschool programs (no pull-ups)
  • Please inform us of any allergies, medical conditions, or special needs

🤝 Financial Assistance

We are committed to making our programs accessible to all families regardless of financial situation. The following assistance options are available:

Sliding Scale Tuition

Tuition is calculated based on family income and household size. Families earning below 80% of the area median income qualify for reduced rates. We accept families at all income levels.

Scholarships & Grants

  • Stone Soup Scholarship Fund: Need-based scholarships covering up to 100% of tuition
  • First 5 California: Subsidies for qualifying families with children ages 0-5
  • CCSA (Child Care Subsidy Agency): Government assistance for working families
  • Local Community Grants: Seasonal grants available through community partners

Payment Plans

We offer flexible payment options to help families manage tuition costs:

  • Monthly payment plans (no additional fee)
  • Quarterly payments (5% discount)
  • Annual payment (10% discount)

Eligibility & Fees

Three simple steps to join Stone Soup Fresno.

1

See if your family meets program requirements.

Stone Soup Fresno is a community benefit organization providing preschool, playgroup, and cultural education programs. Most of our families pay minimal or no fees. We offer scholarships and financial assistance to qualifying families.

We offer sliding scale tuition based on family income and size. To learn about specific income requirements, please contact us directly.

2

Make sure your child is ready to join the Stone Soup Fresno family.

Program Eligibility

  • Preschool: Ages 3-5 years old
  • Playgroup: Ages 2-5 years old
  • Learn Hmong: Open to all ages and families

Ready to apply? You will need:

  • Current physical (most programs require this)
  • Updated immunization records
  • Birth certificate or passport for your child
  • Proof of residency
  • Income verification (for sliding scale tuition)

Other requirements may apply. Contact us for a complete list of required documents for your specific program.

3

Get in touch!

We are here to help. Reach out to learn about eligibility, schedule a tour, or request an application packet.

How to Apply

  • Step 1: Contact us by phone or email to express interest
  • Step 2: Schedule a tour of our facility
  • Step 3: Request and complete an application packet
  • Step 4: Submit application and required documents in person

Please note that we do not accept enrollment applications through this website. You must request an application in person or by contacting us directly.

Ready to Get Started?

Contact us today to schedule a tour and request an application.

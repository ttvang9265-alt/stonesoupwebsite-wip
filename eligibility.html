Eligibility & Fees

Three simple steps to join Stone Soup Fresno.

Step 1

See if your family meets household income requirements.

Stone Soup Fresno is a community benefit organization providing preschool, playgroup, and cultural education programs. Most of our families pay minimal or no fees. We offer scholarships and financial assistance to qualifying families.

Income Guidelines

Contact us for current household income requirements.

We use sliding scale tuition based on family size and income.

Step 2

Make sure your child is ready to join the Stone Soup Fresno family.

Your child must be:

  • Preschool: 3-5 years old
  • Playgroup: 2-5 years old
  • Learn Hmong: All ages welcome

Ready to apply? You will need:

  • Current physical
  • Updated immunization records
  • Birth certificate or passport
  • Income verification

Contact us for a complete list of required documents.

Step 3

Get in touch!

We are here to help. Reach out to learn about eligibility, schedule a tour, or request an application packet.

Contact Us Facebook Page

Ready to Get Started?

Please note that we do not accept enrollment applications through this website. You must request an application in person or by contacting us directly.
